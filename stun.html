Who We Are


Turi itsinda ry’abantu bafite intego yo gufasha buri wese kubona amahirwe yo kwiga,kwidagadura no gutera imbere. Turizera ko buri muntu afite ubushobozi bwo kugera kure mu buzima bwe, kandi tugaharanira kumushyigikira binyuze mu mahugurwa, imyidagaduro, n’ubufasha butandukanye. Turakora ku buntu, kandi intego yacu ni ukurema umuryango wunze ubumwe, usangiye ubunararibonye n’ubumenyi.
